First-Person Shooter

You see through the character's eyes. Maximum immersion. Classic DOOM / Quake feel. Most demanding technically — needs solid raycasting or full 3D engine.

Third-Person Shooter

Camera sits behind/above the character. You see your player. Great for character design payoff. Think Fortnite, Gears of War. Easier to implement than FPS.

Top-Down / Twin-Stick

Bird's eye view. Move with one stick, aim with another. Think Hotline Miami, Enter the Gungeon. Easiest to implement well. Still visually stunning with shaders and lighting.
