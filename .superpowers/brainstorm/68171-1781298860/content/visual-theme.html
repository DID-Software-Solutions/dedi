What's the visual theme?

Sets the art direction, enemy types, weapons, level design — everything flows from this

🚀
SCI-FI
NEON · CHROME · PLASMA

Sci-Fi / Space

Neon lights, alien corridors, energy weapons. Think Halo, Doom 2016, Quake. Great for particle effects and bloom — technically fun to build.

💀
HORROR
DARK · GORE · DREAD

Horror / Survival

Dark corridors, monsters, scarce ammo. Think Doom original, FEAR, Outlast with guns. Fog of war, dynamic shadows, and sound design do heavy lifting.

🎯
MILITARY
TACTICAL · GRITTY · REALISTIC

Military / Tactical

Modern warfare, realistic weapons. Think Call of Duty, CS:GO. Familiar to players, but competitive space is crowded. Needs solid hit detection above all.

⚡
STYLIZED
BOLD · COLORFUL · ARCADY

Stylized / Arcade

Bold colors, exaggerated everything. Think Valorant, Borderlands, Ultrakill. Forgiving art pipeline — easier to make look good without AAA assets.
