Rendering Features (Babylon.js)

  • PBR materials — metal/roughness on all surfaces
  • Dynamic shadows — sun + muzzle flash as light sources
  • Screen-space ambient occlusion (SSAO2)
  • Bloom post-process on emissive surfaces (muzzle flash, enemy glow)
  • Depth of field (subtle — focused on crosshair distance)
  • Fog for atmosphere + performance (limits draw distance)
  • Particle systems: muzzle flash, blood splatter, explosion
