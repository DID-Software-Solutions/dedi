Linear or branching missions with objectives. Story progression, unlocks. More content to create, but clear beginning/end. Think COD single player.

Arena / Deathmatch

Single map, kill enemies, beat your score or time. Pure skill loop. Easiest to scope — one great map, great feel. Classic Quake/Doom arena.
